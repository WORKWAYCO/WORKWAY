# Chrome Web Store Listing

## Extension Name
WORKWAY Zoom Sync

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Sync Zoom session cookies for WORKWAY Meeting Intelligence. Enables automatic meeting transcription to Notion.

## Detailed Description
WORKWAY Zoom Sync enables the Meeting Intelligence workflow by securely syncing your Zoom session cookies.

**What it does:**
- Captures your Zoom authentication cookies (including httpOnly cookies that websites cannot access)
- Securely uploads them to your WORKWAY account
- Auto-syncs every 6 hours while Chrome is running

**Why it's needed:**
Zoom's API doesn't provide access to meeting transcripts with full speaker attribution. This extension enables WORKWAY to access your Zoom recordings page and extract transcripts on your behalf.

**Privacy:**
- Only accesses cookies from zoom.us
- Cookies are sent securely to meetings.workway.co
- No browsing history or other data is collected
- You control when syncing happens

**Requirements:**
- Active WORKWAY account
- Zoom account (logged in via browser)

**Setup:**
1. Install the extension
2. Log into Zoom in your browser
3. Click the extension icon
4. Enter your WORKWAY User ID
5. Click "Sync Now"
