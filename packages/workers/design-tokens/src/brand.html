Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use light variants on dark backgrounds
  • Use dark variants on light backgrounds
  • Maintain clearspace equal to icon height
  • Use SVG for web, PNG for documents

Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Rotate the logo
  • Add shadows or effects
  • Change the logo colors
  • Place on busy backgrounds
